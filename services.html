Safety & Ethical Standards

At Hiru's Turtle Vibe, the safety of our guests and the wellbeing of marine life are our top priorities. We adhere to strict safety protocols and ethical guidelines to ensure a responsible and sustainable experience.

Safety First

Comprehensive briefings, quality equipment, and constant supervision throughout the experience.

Ethical Wildlife Interaction

We maintain safe distances, use only approved feeding methods, and never disturb nesting sites.

Environmental Care

We practice leave-no-trace principles and actively participate in beach clean-up initiatives.
