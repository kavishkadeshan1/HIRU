Booking Information

Contact us via phone, WhatsApp, email, or through our contact form. We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak season.

The best time for turtle encounters is early morning (7-9 AM) or late afternoon (3-5 PM). We operate year-round, but visibility is best during the dry season from December to April.

We accept cash (LKR or USD), mobile payments, and bank transfers. Full payment is required at the time of booking. Cancellations 24+ hours in advance receive a full refund.

Bring: swimwear, towel, sunscreen (reef-safe), waterproof camera, change of clothes, and an adventurous spirit! We provide all snorkeling equipment.
