About Us

Connecting people with the ocean's wonders

Meet Hiru

Hiru's Turtle Vibe was born from a deep love for the ocean and a passion for sharing its magic with others. Guided by Hiru, whose warm smile and sunny spirit light up every trip, we offer an authentic and ethical snorkeling experience in the beautiful coastal town of Thalaramba.

With years of experience in marine conservation and tourism, Hiru has developed a unique approach that prioritizes the wellbeing of sea turtles and the preservation of their natural habitat. Every interaction is carefully managed to ensure minimal impact while maximizing the joy and wonder of these encounters.

What sets us apart is not just our expertise, but our genuine love for what we do. When you join Hiru's Turtle Vibe, you're not just a tourist – you become part of our ocean-loving family.

Our Core Values

What guides everything we do

Ethical Practices

We prioritize the safety and wellbeing of sea turtles and marine life. All our interactions are conducted with the utmost respect for nature and follow strict ethical guidelines.

Authentic Experience

We believe in creating genuine connections – with nature, with the ocean, and with each other. Our small group sizes ensure personalized attention and meaningful experiences.

Quality & Safety

Your safety is our top priority. We provide high-quality equipment, thorough safety briefings, and constant supervision to ensure a safe and enjoyable adventure.
